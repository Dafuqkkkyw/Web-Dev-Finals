About Us

We are a group of students who are also gamers, focused on creating a platform for Valorant fans. We provide an Agent Tier List to help players understand the best agents and their roles, and offer official Valorant merchandise like stickers, plushies, and keychains.

Our goal is to make Valorant more enjoyable for the community, whether it is improving gameplay or collecting cool items.
